Information

A large management system to manage partner, people, companies, employees, vehicles and transactions, exceeding 30,000 lines of code. it has 4 different levels of users Super Admin, Admin, Finacial Team and Mandoub. with automated notifications sent at specific times using Cron With privileges given to each type of user.

Super Admin: can add admin and partner companies, and reset passwords for admins only this account was minted to be created to be managed by CTO.

Admin: are able to add, manage or remove employees, vehicles, clients and companies. They have the control to create Mandoub and Financial Accounts for their team. According to the partner company they registered under. They can create and manage transactions and set the price for each type of transaction. Admins will also have the ability to upload different documents,such as QID, Company Registration Papers, different types of insurance documents, etc.

Financial Team Account: Can only manage transactions and their prices or update transaction info.

Mandoubs: have a very similar right as admin but, they can't create accounts and, they are unable to see the prices of anything.

The initial project was completed within 4 months, however, due to client requests many features are still added day by day.
